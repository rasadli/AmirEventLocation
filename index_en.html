About Us

Amir Event Location is the epitome of elegance and exclusivity. Our magnificent hall offers space for up to 250 guests and can be flexibly divided if needed, making it the perfect setting for both grand celebrations and intimate gatherings.

Whether you are planning a stylish wedding, a festive engagement, a representative corporate event, or a special anniversary – we create the ideal atmosphere tailored to your wishes. With great attention to detail, versatile design options, and exquisite decorations, our hall transforms into an ambiance that makes your celebration truly unforgettable.

A special highlight is our modern photo booth, seamlessly integrated into the venue. It gives your guests the opportunity to capture unique memories – charming, stylish, and with a touch of fun.

Amir Event Location combines luxurious flair with personalized service, turning your event into an experience that will be remembered for years to come.
